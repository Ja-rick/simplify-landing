About This Product

Simplify is a Chrome extension designed to take complicated language and instantly break it down into simpler, easier-to-read text using AI.

Perfect for students, ADHD users, ESL readers, and anyone who doesn’t have time to decipher walls of text.

What is Simplify?

Simplify is a browser extension built for everyone — whether you're a student knee-deep in research, a busy professional juggling too much, or just someone curious and hungry to learn.

With just one click, Simplify takes any block of text on any website and breaks it down into a clean, readable summary. No fluff, no confusion — just quick comprehension.

Whether you're skimming legal docs, decoding Reddit threads, or trying to understand a complex concept, Simplify makes essential reading fast, clear, and optimized.

Privacy Policy

Simplify does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. The extension only sends selected text to an external API in order to simplify it.

This data is not stored or shared with any third parties. API usage is monitored solely for billing and rate-limiting purposes. No user identity, activity, or browsing history is recorded.
